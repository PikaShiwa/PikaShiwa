About Quabbin Regional School District

Quabbin Regional School District is a public school district located in central Massachusetts, dedicated to providing quality education to the communities of Barre, Hardwick, Hubbardston, New Braintree, and Oakham. The district operates several schools that serve students from kindergarten through 12th grade.

Schools within the Quabbin Regional School District

  • Ruggles Lane Elementary School
  • Hardwick Elementary School
  • Hubbardston Center School
  • New Braintree Grade School
  • Oakham Center School
  • Quabbin Regional Middle/High School

Quabbin Regional School District Programs

The Quabbin Regional School District offers a variety of programs designed to meet the diverse needs of its students. These include:

  • STEM Programs: Focused on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
  • Arts Education: Music, visual arts, theater, and dance programs.
  • Athletics: Soccer, basketball, track, and cross-country teams.
  • Special Education Services: Tailored programs to support students with unique learning needs.

Why Choose Quabbin Regional School District?

Quabbin offers a wide variety of academic programs, from AP courses to specialized electives. The district is known for its strong community involvement, commitment to academic excellence, and diverse extracurricular activities. With dedicated staff and a supportive educational environment, Quabbin prepares students for college, careers, and beyond.

Academic Excellence at Quabbin

Our students consistently perform well on standardized tests, including the MCAS, and are prepared for success in higher education and professional life. Quabbin Regional High School students have been accepted to top universities and colleges across the country.

Community Engagement

Quabbin Regional School District prides itself on fostering a strong sense of community. With frequent updates on local school events and meetings, parents, students, and faculty are always in touch. Whether it's through the Parent-Teacher Association or community events, there's always a way to get involved.
