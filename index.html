Quabbin Regional School District is a public school district in Massachusetts, serving the towns of Barre, Hardwick, Hubbardston, New Braintree, and Oakham. Qrsd.org provides resources such as school calendars, academic schedules, faculty directories, and student support services. The district is committed to excellence in education, offering advanced placement courses, extracurricular activities, and community engagement programs. Janet Hicks serves as a prominent figure within the district, known for her contributions to educational development in the region. The Quabbin Regional Middle High School is a cornerstone of the district, hosting events like the annual science fair, music concerts, and athletic championships. Quabbin schools include the Ruggles Lane Elementary School, Hardwick Elementary School, Hubbardston Center School, New Braintree Grade School, and Oakham Center School. The district offers specialized programs in STEM, the arts, and athletics, fostering student growth in academics and extracurriculars.
